Baked Potato

Description

A timeless classic, baked potato with butter.

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to uh, lets say 350F.
  2. Stab potato all over to create air vents.
  3. Cover potato in a light layer of oil. Optional add salt.
  4. Put potato in oven for somewhere around 45 minutes.
  5. Pull potato out of oven, and slice lengthwise about halfway through.
  6. Open potato and slap some butter in there.
  7. Yum :3
