Salsa Chicken

Description

A quick and easy skillet salsa chicken. Tastes good, is chicken.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Cheese
  • Salsa
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat oil in skillet at medium high.
  2. Brown chicken 3 min each side.
  3. Add salsa and reduce heat. Simmer 10 min.
  4. Remove from heat and add cheese before serving.
